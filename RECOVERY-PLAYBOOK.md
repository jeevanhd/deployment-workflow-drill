# Recovery Playbook

Document the actions to take when a deployment fails.

## Failed deployment response

- What immediate actions must the team take?
- Who owns the incident?
- How is the failure communicated?

## Rollback triggers

- What conditions trigger an automatic rollback?
- What conditions trigger a manual rollback?
- Who authorizes rollback execution?

## Escalation process

- Which teams are notified when a failed deployment occurs?
- What escalation path is followed?
- What is the expected response time?

## Communication process

- How are stakeholders informed?
- What channels are used?
- What information is shared during and after the event?
